ANSEL ADAMS

is the most famous nature photographer in history – he’s also well known for his environmental conservation efforts. Ansel Adams was commissioned to capture the national parks, and his most famous landscape photographs are from the Yosemite National Park. His legacy goes beyond the natural world – Ansel Adams advocated for straight photography and founded the Group f/64. He also developed the zone system that is used by many other famous nature photographers.
